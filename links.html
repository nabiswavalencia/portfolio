Hack 001:Zero Hunger

The topic handled was Zero hunger and the participants were placed into groups that were to come up with solutions using technology on how to manage the problem. It was a great opportunity to interact with other brilliant minds and enhance our critical thinking and problem solving skills.

Participants placed in groups
to come up with a solution

It was beginner friendly since each group was distributed equally with at least a beginner and advanced in each. I learnt a lot on Web in terms of the practical aspect and resources to be used. I was introduced to several tools like tailwind,kitwind,l and pixabay that might come in handy in terms of speed and extensive research work.

At the end of the event we had presentation of the group works. I found that part of the session very helpful since we shared ideas on how each group looked at the problem and the trajectory they took to solve it. Several ideas came through and the questions asked on the few nitty gritties left out also helped in expanding our view on the solutions. The teams were judged according to the idea brought up and its applicability. The winning team was rewarded though at the end we all gained so much from the event.
